Who We Are

The Learning Camp Initiative is a nonprofit organization committed to helping children in underserved communities acquire foundational literacy and numeracy skills. We implement the Teaching at the Right Level (TaRL) methodology and the Language Learning from Familiar to Formal (L2F2) approach to ensure that every child, regardless of background, can succeed in school.

Our Mission

Ensuring quality foundational learning for every Nigerian child through inclusive approaches.

Our Vision

A future where every Nigerian child completes primary school with the ability to read and do basic math. A Nigeria where every child can read and do basic math before leaving primary school.

Our Core Values

  • Equity in Education
  • Child-Centered Learning
  • Accountability
  • Community Engagement
  • Innovation in Teaching

Impact Areas

350 students currently participating in the pilot program

20+ volunteer facilitators trained

Significant improvement in basic literacy observed within 3 weeks

Strong foundational base for larger-scale implementation.
